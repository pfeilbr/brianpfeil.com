# Music Sound Touch
    
  <p><img src="images/Icon-72@2x.png" alt="" /></p>

<p>Fun and easy interactive music app designed by parents for young children who enjoy the look and sound of musical instruments. A terrific collection of real musical instrument pictures and sounds that play with one easy tap. You&rsquo;ll also hear the name of the instrument along with the sound. It will keep those little ones busy for quite a while!</p>

<div style="clear: both"></div>


<h2>Features</h2>

<ul>
<li>Easy to use</li>
<li>Real Photos</li>
<li>High Quality Sound</li>
<li>Narration of instrument name</li>
</ul>
