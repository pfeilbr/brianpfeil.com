### Scratchpad

The scratchpad is a temporary working space for keeping commonly used shapes for easy access.  See [How to use the Scratchpad?](https://desk.draw.io/support/solutions/articles/16000042367)

exported aws-scratchpad @ [`aws-scratchpad-panel-export.xml`](aws-scratchpad-panel-export.xml).  

> Note that the scratchpad is stored in the local storage of the browser, which means it's contents are deleted if cookies are cleared. The scratchpad is automatically saved between each change.

> You can export the scratchpad as a draw.io library from that dialog, if you want to store it more permanently or share it with others.
